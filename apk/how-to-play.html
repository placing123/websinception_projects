Additional points to remember

Along with the above system of points and the tables attached, you must also remember a few more factors.

  • Strike rate is applicable only for strike rate below 70 runs per 100 balls.

  • In case of run-outs involving 3 or more players from the fielding team, the run-out points will only be awarded and counted for the last 2 players involved.

  • Substitutes on the field will typically not count for any points. Although the case is different for "Concussion substitutes." Your team will be awarded points for the relevant contributions they make on the field in their case.

  • Although, concussion substitutes are awarded only 2 points for making an appearance in the match instead of the regular 4 points that are allotted to a player on your team who is present in the starting 11 squad in real-time.

It must also be noted that the transfer of players in real life during the intervening period between scheduled updates will not be reflected in player rosters until the next scheduled update.

Please note that although the transferred player may be available in their new team's roster and is also available for your selection in the intervening period during a contest, they will not be awarded any points during such a game.
